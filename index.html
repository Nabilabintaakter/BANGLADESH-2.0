National Pride Takes New Name: 'Bangladesh Satellite-1' Operational Name Finalized

Date: 05/01/2025

Time: 10.25 PM

The nation's first geostationary communications and broadcasting satellite, previously known as Bangabandhu Satellite-1, has been officially renamed "Bangladesh Satellite-1" (BS-1). The decision, approved by the Chief Adviser and announced by the Ministry of Posts, Telecommunications, and Information Technology, finalizes the renaming process begun earlier this year.

Earth Observation Satellite Deal Finalized: 'Bangabandhu-2' Slated for 2027 Launch

Date: 14/11/2025

Time: 1.15 PM

The Bangladesh Satellite Company Limited (BSCL) announced today the finalization of a multi-million-dollar deal with French aerospace giant Airbus Defence and Space SAS for the manufacturing of the country's second satellite, tentatively named Bangabandhu-2 (BB-2). Following an earlier Letter of Intent, this agreement cements Bangladesh's foray into the field of Earth Observation (EO) satellites.

Tech Investment Summit Concludes with Focus on Deep Tech and Space-Tech Education

Date: 15/08/2025

Time: 4.05 PM

The Bangladesh Investment Summit wrapped up its three-day agenda with a strong emphasis on attracting capital into "Deep Tech"—technologies founded on tangible scientific discoveries and engineering innovations—including space technology, artificial intelligence, and advanced robotics.
